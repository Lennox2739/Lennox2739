Data Flow

ways of data flow

Simplex

Commmunication is unidirectional. Only one of the two devices in a link can trasmit

Duplex

Both stations can transmit but not at the same time. When one device is sending the other can Only receive but not at the same time.

Full duplex

Both stations can transmit and receive simulataneously. Here, signals going in one direction, share link capacity with signals going the direction. Sharing can occur in two ways. Either the link must contain two for sending and another for receiving/capacity of the channel is divided into signals travelling in both directions.
